Welcome!

Full-Stack Developer, Quirky Jewelry Collector, Foodie, Musical Junkie, Idle Writer, Card Player, INFJ



I am currently enrolled in Northwestern's Coding Bootcamp program and learning the fundamentals of full-stack web development. I am eager to continue learning and apply my previous experience and knowledge in a technical field.

My background is in social science research and I have always been "data-oriented," however the driving force in my career has been helping improve the lives of people with neuro-developmental disabilities. I have interests in disability advocacy, research, and community engagement, and hope to use my newfound technical knowledge to make a larger impact toward these interests.
